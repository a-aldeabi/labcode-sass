Course Overview

This course introduces you to the fundamentals of User Experience (UX) design. You'll learn how to create intuitive and user-friendly interfaces by understanding key principles such as user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing. Through hands-on exercises and real-world examples, you will gain the skills necessary to design seamless digital experiences that meet user needs and improve satisfaction. Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your existing skills, this course will provide valuable insights into the world of UX design.
